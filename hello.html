Sentiment Analysis using Natural Language Processing

Our project focuses on analyzing textual data to determine the underlying sentiment, such as positive or negative. By leveraging advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques, we preprocess, tokenize, and classify text inputs to extract meaningful insights. This system can be applied to social media posts, customer reviews, or feedback analysis to understand public opinion and trends. The model is trained on labeled datasets to ensure accuracy and adaptability across various domains, providing valuable insights for decision-making and strategy.
